About Us

Founded in 2003, PainterSmith has established itself as a go-to creative resource for clients who seek high caliber advertising solutions without conventional agency layers.



Our core philosophy is that creativity is a vital component of successful advertising. Our streamlined approach allows us to stay flexible and competitive in a challenging economy.

With experience in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments and expertise in a wide range of disciplines, PainterSmith strives to bring integrity, ingenuity, and inspiration to every aspect of our services.
